About Atelier Saucier

Breathing new life into forgotten fabrics

Sustainability plus giving back make Atelier Saucier a Boon favorite. They thoughtfully source surplus premium fabrics from L.A. fashion houses and repurpose them into table linens, runners and napkins. Their small-batch production process reduces waste, and they give back to wellness initiatives in Los Angeles.
