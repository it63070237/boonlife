Boon partnered with Territory because of their dedication to artisans in India and their commitment to craft. The Cotton Square Pillow Cover in Clay with Frayed Edges exemplifies the power of goods to do good, and evokes the rich history of India. In the 1900's, Mahatma Gandhi led the social reform movement and fostered a renewed appreciation and interest in the country's well-respected weaving traditions. He believed that teaching people to spin and weave would help overcome the wealth divide and increase the country's independence from foreign powers. Territory works with partners in South India who are committed to ensuring safe and fair work environments, improving the standard of living, and increasing literacy rates in the region. A dense cotton chambray, this pillow is soft but sturdy. Cover only, does not include insert.
